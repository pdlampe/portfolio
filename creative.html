About Paul

Web developer with experience in government public affairs, graphic design and advertising. Certificate from the University of Kansas in full-stack web technologies including JavaScript, CSS, HTML, Bootstrap and Node.js. Seven years of experience working on advertising campaigns for environmental and social services programs. My background in strategic communications and graphic design gives me the foundation to address the needs of specific audiences and identify how they would engage with a website or app. Passionate about creating well organized, concise and engaging websites with other creative and analytical professionals. My keen eye for detail and an ability to focus on aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly design would make me a strong addition to companies and teams focused on building better website experiences.
